This project is a digital manifestation of my belief in open-source philosophy and technical excellence. It distances itself from classic portfolio molds, designed to evoke the feeling of a "developer's workspace".

## Architectural Approach

- **Next.js App Router**: High performance and scalability.
- **MDX Content Management**: Static, flexible, and fast content structure.
- **Zod Validation**: Content type safety.

## Design Philosophy
Minimalist, unpretentious but confident. It offers a smooth experience with a very subtle paper texture and silent animations.
